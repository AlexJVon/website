About Me

IT Graduate · Game Developer · Outdoor Enthusiast

I'm Alexander Vonderau, a 21-year-old IT graduate from the University of North Florida. I grew up hiking and working outdoors, which taught me discipline, adaptability, and how to stay calm under pressure.

I'm interested in roles at the intersection of technology and creative problem solving. I'm also interested in outdoor roles of any sort.
